About EcoYaan

EcoYaan helps you save money, reduce emissions, and build community through shared rides.

Unlike other apps, EcoYaan is a 0% commission ride-sharing platform. Drivers keep 100% of their earnings.

🌟 Why People Love Us

  • Save on Daily Commute Costs

    Drivers set their own price. Riders split the fare. There’s no middleman taking a commission. Everyone wins.

  • Go Green, Together

    Less cars, more savings. Every shared ride cuts emissions — making your commute part of the solution.

  • Build Real Connections

    Travel with people from your campus, workplace, or city who share your hobbies, interests, or even podcast taste.

  • Safety Comes First

    All users are verified through LinkedIn or Google. You can view ride history, ratings, and mutual connections before accepting.

🎯 Who Can Use Carpool Connect?

Features You'll Love

  • 🚫 Zero Commission Model

    We don’t take a cut from ride fares. Drivers pay a flat subscription — which means you earn more per trip.

  • 📅 Smart Scheduling

    Whether it’s daily or one-off rides, our dynamic scheduling adapts to your routine.

  • 🔐 Verified Accounts

    Only users with social logins (LinkedIn/Google) can access the platform, increasing trust and accountability.

  • 🏆 Rider Rewards (Coming Soon)

    Earn points for frequent carpooling and redeem for partner discounts, events, or eco-friendly gear.

🛣️ How Carpool Connect Works

Step 1 – Post or Search Rides

Open the app to post your trip as a driver or search for available rides as a rider. Filter by location, time, and travel frequency.

Step 2 – Match with Compatible Riders

We connect you with commuters who share similar interests, destinations, and safety preferences — because sharing a ride should feel safe and social.

Step 3 – Travel Smart

Meet at the pickup point, enjoy the ride, split costs, and reduce your carbon footprint. No commission, no complicated fees — just a simple, smart carpool.
